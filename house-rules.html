House Rules

For many people our place is something very special. No ingrain wallpaper, no drywall... but an old house made of adobe and wood.

This means:

  • … an original half-timbered house and a good room climate
  • … minimalist and sometimes unfamiliar facilities
  • … the house was expanded by our father - and we are planning to continue it this way
  • … in winter you can hear the snowfall against the wall when in complete silence
  • … the house has hosted many people and some of it has left its marks


What needs to be taken into account:

  • … we are part of a small village community - please be respectful with everyone and everything
  • … we welcome everyone who wants to live in an open and diverse society, as we do.
  • … the heart of the house are the wood ovens. On cold days you will HAVE to operate them yourself!
  • … if you do not know how to do this please tell us in advance. We are happy to explain it to you.
  • … Candles and other open fires are not allowed in the whole house!
  • … Camp fires and BBQs are only allowed upon agreement with us!
  • … in spring hibernating insects wake up in our beams. We cannot and do not want to take away this save haven from them.
  • … wood transmits sound well. There is a noise dampening built into the floor but it does not prevent sounds completely.
  • … our water is drinkable but extremely calcareous / hard. There is a water filter jug available for you to use.
